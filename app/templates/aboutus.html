Intended Purpose

The intended purpose of this app is to help out those struggling with mental health issues by providing resources such as videos, articles, and therapy.



Interactivity Description

For each teenager who is struggling with mental health, they will use our app by first answering the setup questions. They will then have the features they choose from that survey on the app such as daily tasks, videos to watch, and activities to do. By doing the taskss, they will be able to track their progress so they can see how their mental health is affected by the daily tasks.
